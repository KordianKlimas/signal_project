The sequence diagram displays 3 possible situations.
1. The patients continuous  health monitoring.
The data is continuously gathered for each patient. It's analyzed by the alert generator with use of patients history and conditions.
If the alert generator finds the state of patient to be in danger, it will alert the staff.
The staff later gives feedback on whether the alert was needed. The alert generator analyzes given feedback and applies new history of patient states.

2. The staff  is able to update, or add new history or conditions to the data center. The alert generator will analyze new data.
3. The staff can inform the alert generator  about occurance of health risk state of patient if the alert generator did not recognized it as risk in past.
